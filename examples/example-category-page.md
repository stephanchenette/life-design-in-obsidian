# My Quality of Life

## Quotes

"The point is not to look successful. The point is to actually enjoy being alive."

## Premise

Quality of life is not an afterthought after work is done. It is part of the structure of a good life. Environment, rhythm, adventure, and beauty shape behavior more than abstract intention does.

## Vision

A strong version of this category means:

- a home that supports reading, conversation, and rest
- regular time outside
- recurring adventure that is easy to say yes to
- fewer passive defaults and more deliberate experiences

## Purpose

Without this category, life starts feeling like maintenance. A better quality of life creates energy for relationships, work, health, and creativity.

## Strategy

- Redesign one physical space this quarter.
- Schedule one meaningful outdoor activity this month.
- Replace one low-value default habit with one high-value experience.

## Habits

- Weekly walk without devices
- Monthly local adventure

## Notes

- The best next move is often environmental, not motivational.
